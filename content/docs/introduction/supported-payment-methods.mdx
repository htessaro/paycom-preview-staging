Pay.com provides a comprehensive range of payment options to ensure you can reach customers globally while providing a localized checkout experience. 
This page lists the available payment methods and their typical configurations.

Pay.com supports three primary categories of payment methods:
* **Global Card Networks:** Standard credit and debit cards.
* **Digital Wallets:** Modern, one-touch mobile and web payment solutions.
* **Local Payment Methods:** Region-specific alternatives like bank transfers and ACH.

## Global Cards
Accept all major credit and debit card brands through a single integration.

| Card Brand | Supported Regions | Key Features |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Visa** | Global | Credit, Debit, Prepaid, 3DS2 supported |
| **Mastercard** | Global | Credit, Debit, Prepaid, 3DS2 supported |
| **American Express** | Global | High-value transactions, 3DS2 supported |
| **Discover / Diners** | Global / US | Standard credit processing |
| **UnionPay** | Global / Asia | Growing international acceptance |

**Configurable Options:**
* **3D Secure (3DS2):** Can be enabled to reduce fraud and shift liability.
* **Card Account Updater:** Automatically updates expired or replaced card details.
* **Prepaid Card Support:** Toggle whether to allow or block prepaid/gift card types.

## Digital Wallets
Wallets reduce friction by allowing customers to pay using stored credentials on their devices.

| Wallet | Platform | Description |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Apple Pay** | iOS / Safari | Secure biometric authentication (FaceID/TouchID). |
| **Google Pay** | Android / Chrome | Fast checkout using cards saved to a Google Account. |
| **PayPal** | Global / Web | One of the most trusted global digital wallets. |

**Configurable Options:**
* **Wallet Button Styling:** Customize the size and color of the button to match your UI.
* **Billing/Shipping Info:** Choose whether to request the customer's contact details from the wallet provider.

## Local & Alternative Payment Methods (APMs)
Expand your reach in specific markets by offering the methods your customers use most locally.

### North America
* **ACH Direct Debit:** Secure bank-to-bank transfers in the US. Requires a routing and account number.

### Europe
* **SEPA Direct Debit:** Simplified bank transfers across the Eurozone.
* **Local Redirects:** Support for regional favorites like iDEAL (Netherlands), Bancontact (Belgium), and P24 (Poland).

### Latin America
* **Local Methods:** Support for Pix (Brazil), OXXO (Mexico), and Boleto (Brazil) to reach unbanked customers.

## Possible Configurations
Pay.com provides flexible settings that allow you to manage your payment experience directly through your account, giving you control over how you accept and process payments.

### 1. Payment Method Management
You can control your checkout options instantly through the Pay.com Dashboard without needing to change any website code.
* Easily add or remove specific payment options as your business grows.
* Configure your settings so that customers are automatically shown the most relevant local payment methods based on where they are shopping.

### 2. Transaction Handling
You can decide how payments are processed to best fit your business model and fulfillment process.
* Choose whether to charge the customer immediately at checkout or authorize the amount first and capture the funds later.
* Enable options for customers to securely save their payment details for faster one-click checkouts or ongoing subscription billing.

### 3. Security and Fraud Protection
Manage your risk and compliance levels through built-in security features that protect both you and your customers.
* Every payment method uses secure tokenization so that sensitive data is safely stored by Pay.com, removing the security burden from your business.
* Set up specific preferences to flag or block suspicious transactions based on your own risk comfort level.
