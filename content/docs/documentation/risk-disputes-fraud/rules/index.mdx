title: Rules overview
description: Configure pre- and post-authorization rules to block, flag,
  or authenticate charges based on conditions you define.
---

Pay.com's rules engine evaluates each charge against conditions you define
and applies a configured action automatically. Rules run either before
authorization (payment rules) or after authorization (review rules). You
configure and manage all rules in the Pay.com dashboard.

Explore the guides below to set up and manage rules based on your workflow:
