**Pay.com** is a unified payment gateway and processor that enables businesses to accept online payments securely. Whether you are a developer seeking granular control or a business owner looking for a "plug-and-play" solution, our platform scales with your needs.

### Flexible integration methods
Our ecosystem is built around three primary integration paths, allowing you to choose the level of technical involvement that suits your workflow:

* **REST API**: Build a fully automated experience by managing sessions, customers, and payments directly through your backend.
* **Client SDK**: Accelerate frontend development by invoking secure payment sessions and managing client secrets with minimal configuration.
* **Merchant Dashboard**: Utilize a no-code interface to manually configure your account, style checkout pages, and monitor real-time transaction data.

### Core features and benefits
We prioritize transparency and security to help you streamline global commerce. By leveraging our **PCI DSS Level 1** compliant infrastructure, you can bypass the complexity of traditional merchant accounts and go live quickly.

* **Fast Onboarding:** A dedicated team assists with a quick signup process.
* **Modular System:** As your business grows, additional capabilities can be added to the system.
* **Global Reach:** Accept payments from customers worldwide shortly after setup.
* **Customizable checkout**: Align the payment experience with your brand aesthetics using our styling tools.
* **Pay links**: Generate secure, shareable links for email, SMS, or WhatsApp to collect payments without a website.
* **Real-time analytics**: Track growth and performance through an intuitive data dashboard.
* **Transparent pricing**: Operate with a predictable flat-fee structure and no hidden costs.

## Onboarding process
Setting up your account is a streamlined five-step journey from initial contact to your first transaction:

1. **Contact sales**: Visit [Pay.com](https://pay.com/) to initiate your account request via our [Contact Sales](https://pay.com/contact-sales) page.
2. **Verify your identity**: Click the activation link sent to your email to begin the business registration process.
3. **Submit documentation**: Complete a brief form regarding your business model and provide the necessary compliance documents.
4. **Application review**: Our team will audit your submission to ensure everything meets regulatory standards.
5. **Go live**: Once approved, you can immediately begin processing payments and exploring the API.
