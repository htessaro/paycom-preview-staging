Pay.com is a payment gateway and orchestration platform that gives businesses access to a global ecosystem of payment methods, acquiring banks, and fraud tools through a single integration.

The traditional approach to payment acceptance means maintaining separate relationships with a gateway, a processor, and each payment method provider. Pay.com replaces all of that with one connection. You integrate once, and the platform handles routing, compliance, and payment method support behind the scenes.

<Callout type="info">
**Connect once. Works everywhere.** One Pay.com integration gives your business access to every supported payment method, all your acquiring relationships, and the routing logic that keeps your authorization rates high — without any additional integrations.
</Callout>

## About this section

This section gives decision makers a complete picture of what Pay.com does, how it works, and what sets it apart before involving a technical team. If you're a developer who already has credentials and is ready to integrate, the [API reference](/api-reference/get-started) and [payment guides](/documentation/payments/guides/create-a-charge) are the fastest path forward.

Pay.com documentation is built for both human readers and AI tools. Every page is available as plain markdown by appending `.md` to any URL, and the Pay.com [MCP server](/documentation/introduction/mcp) lets AI coding assistants query documentation directly.
