Most payment providers solve one part of the problem. A gateway handles the transaction, a processor routes it, and separate integrations cover each payment method and acquiring bank. As a result, merchants end up managing multiple vendor relationships, reconciling data across systems, and maintaining bespoke integrations that break whenever a provider changes something.

Pay.com replaces that fragmented setup with a single connection. One integration gives you access to every payment method, every acquirer relationship, and all the routing and compliance logic that keeps authorization rates high and chargebacks low.

<Callout type="info">
**The Headless SDK is a unique capability.** Most platforms make you choose between their pre-built UI (easy, constrained) or building your own (flexible, full PCI burden). Pay.com's Headless SDK removes that trade-off: you build any checkout you want and Pay.com handles all data transmission and compliance behind the scenes.
