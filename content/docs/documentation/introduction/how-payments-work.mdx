Pay.com handles the technical behind-the-scenes processing to get your business up and running. This guide covers how our system manages payment methods, routing, and user experience.

You decide which payment methods to accept through the **Pay Dashboard**, where you can add or remove them at any time. 
Pay.com allows you to accept a wide variety of methods, including:
* **Cards:** Credit and debit cards.
* **Transfers:** ACH transfers.
* **Digital Wallets:** PayPal, Google Pay, Apple Pay, and others.

## Transaction lifecycle and routing
Once a payment is created, Pay.com manages the internal statuses and routing to ensure the merchant is informed of the final transaction state.

Our smart routing system is designed to handle failed cards and technical updates:
* **Status Management:** The system updates and tracks statuses such as "expired" for failed attempts.
* **Final State:** Pay.com ensures the merchant receives the final transaction state for every payment.

## Payment options
Customers can complete transactions through multiple interfaces:

### 1. Checkout page
A branded, seamless checkout experience automatically optimized for the customer's device and preferred payment service provider. It features the highest level of PCI-DSS compliance and visible security logos.

### 2. Pay links
Direct links created by the merchant and sent via email or text. Clicking the link takes the customer to a personalized checkout page to complete the transaction.

### 3. Virtual terminal
For businesses accepting payments over the phone, credit card details can be entered manually into the virtual terminal.
